Booklandia in LPSci

Another event that LPSci held for English Month was Booklandia. We got to dress up as our favorite book characters for a day and we even got a chance to go to a parade, where we got to walk around the school's oval. I had a lot of fun, I dressed up as Pansy Parkinson, a side character from Harry Potter from the Slytherin house. It was a truly fun experience, and I'm so excited for who I am going to represent next year! Stay tuned !!
